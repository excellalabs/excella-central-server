Installation

iOS Instructions

  1. Tap the button to download the app.
  2. Once the app finishes downloading, tap to open it. You'll see an "Untrusted Enterprise Developer" message; click Cancel.
  3. Go to Settings > General > Device Management.
  4. Tap "Excella Consulting, Inc."
  5. Tap "Trust Excella Consulting, Inc."
  6. Go back and try to open Excella Central again, it should now be installed!

Android Instructions

  1. Go to Settings > Security, and turn on the "Unknown sources" option.
  2. Tap the button to download the app.
  3. Once the app finishes downloading, click the file to install it.
  4. Try opening the Excella Central app; you should be good to go!
