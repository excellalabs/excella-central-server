Installation

iOS Instructions

  1. From the previous webpage, tap the button to download the iOS app.
  2. Once the app finishes downloading, tap to open it. You'll see an "Untrusted Enterprise Developer" message; click Cancel.
  3. Go to Settings > General > Device Management.
  4. Tap "Excella Consulting, Inc."
  5. Tap "Trust Excella Consulting, Inc."
  6. Go back and try to open Excella Central again, it should now be installed!

Android Instructions (for Android O)

  1. From the previous webpage, tap the button to download the Android app.
  2. Once the app finishes downloading, click the file to install it.
  3. If you have not enabled installing unknown apps from your browser, a pop-up will appear indicating this. Click the "Settings" button on the pop-up.
  4. Toggle the "Allow from this source" option on the Install Unknown Apps permission for your browser.
  5. Install the app.
  6. Try opening the Excella Central app; you should be good to go!

Android Instructions (for other versions)

  1. Go to Settings > Security, and turn on the "Unknown sources" option.
  2. From the previous webpage, tap the button to download the Android app.
  3. Once the app finishes downloading, click the file to install it.
  4. Try opening the Excella Central app; you should be good to go!
